Dear participant,


Please consider participating in the study about bilingualism and cognition. Participation in this study is completely voluntary. You are under no obligation to participate in this study and, if you decide to participate, you may stop at any time. There will be no consequences for deciding not to participate or for deciding to stop participating in the study.

If you decide to participate, you will be asked to fill out the Google form that includes demographic questions and two tests which takes from 10 to 15 minutes to complete. The information you provide during your participation in this study will remain confidential. The results of the study will only be reported in aggregate form and no identifying information will be released. To further safeguard your confidentiality, please do not write your name or any identifying information on the study instruments. The information you provide during the course of this study will not be used for any purpose outside the scope of this research project. All data will be destroyed by 5 May, 2023.

Participation in this study involves minimal risk. You will be asked to share your age and country of origin. However, no other identifying information will be requested. The nature of this study does not lead you to share any sensitive information.

There is no cost to participate in this project.

The study is being conducted by Igor Papuc as a part of fourth-year thesis process at LCC International University. If you have any questions or concerns regarding the study, please contact Igor Papuc, at ipapuc20@students.lcc.lt or Dr. Jennifer Schneider, jschneider@lcc.lt.


Thank you for your time!
